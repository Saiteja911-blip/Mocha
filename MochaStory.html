From Yemen’s Port of Mocha


The name “Mocha” comes from the ancient Yemeni port city of Al Mokha, once famous for exporting some of the world’s best coffee beans. These beans had natural hints of chocolate, inspiring the modern drink we now know as a mocha — a rich mix of espresso, milk, and cocoa.

The Perfect Fusion


Mocha beautifully unites the intensity of espresso with the sweetness of chocolate and the creaminess of steamed milk. It’s both comforting and energizing — a beverage that feels like dessert in a cup while keeping the strength of real coffee.
